Chockingphobia, developed in 2021-2022, is a website developed for the purpose of raising awareness on phagophobia and pseudodysphagia. These two conditions are simply the fears of swallowing and the fears of chocking respectively.They are rare disorders and have no specific treatment. The goal of raising awareness on these issues is to educate the larger community on the two phobias and on the significance of mental health in general.

Chockingphobia is driven by two main values and principles that form the epicentre of entire project :

Care

We believe that care for people suffering with phagophobia, pseudodysphagia and any other type of phobias, is the best that you could provide for them. Showing your care for someone suffering from this condition might be a way of helping them express themselves well. As a result, they may be able to overcome what they are going through and lead a normal life.

Empathy

Before judging people too quickly, try to put yourself in their shoes first. People living with Phagophobia or pseudodysphagia may often fall as victims of bullying or isolation. This may affect their recovery and also bring about more physical or mental impacts. The ability to understand what someone else is going through is key in helping your friend or family member overcome phagophobia and pseudodysphagia. Therefore, we believe in the power of compassion and empathy.
