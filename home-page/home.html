Phobias! Phobias! Phobias!. Phobias are defined as extreme and irrational fears. They are of different types and may sound awkward to some people. For instance, Ablutophobia is the fear of bathing and chloephobia is the fear of newspapers. While some of this processes eg. Bathing and reading newspapers may be usual to someone, they may be also scary to some people. In this page, I will discuss two types of phobias that I had suffered at a personal level. The phobias are phagophobia and pseudodysphagia. While they may have complex names, they simply mean the fear of swallowing and the fear of choking respectively.
