SUPPORT

Below are some points on how you can show support to a loved one who is suffering from phagophobia and pseudodysphagia.



  1. Understand their situation


  2. Show love and care
  3. Encourage them to overcome their fear of swallowing or choking
  4. Do not show them any form of discrimination
  5. Encourage them too seek therapy if the condition persists
  6. Help them through the anxiety stage to prevent them from suffering from depression
  7. Be keen to check out for signs of depression or self-harm
